Software
Platform is our flagship tool. We transform your PDFs into an interactive web app, that doesn't require advanced computer skills to operate.

  • Search for text in your documents using keywords such as drawing titles and equipment tags.

  • Find related documents with a single click.

  • Project documents are automatically linked together. Follow the inter-drawing links (continuations) to see the complete picture.

Scope is automatically extracted from your PDF files.

Comments and pictures can be easily added to a project task by field or office users.

Update your task status to get accurate progress reports.

Get up to the minute status of any field task.

Generate summary and S-curve reports to quickly convey facts.

Services
If it exists on a drawing, we have a process to assemble the information you require.
  • We extract data from PDF documents (e.g.: bill of materials, scope take-offs and quantities) and deliver in an Excel-compatible format.
  • We automate repetitive tasks such as sorting engineering documents by systems, commodities, and area.
