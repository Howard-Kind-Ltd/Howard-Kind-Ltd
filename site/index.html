Services
If it exists on a drawing, we have a process to assemble the information you require.
We extract data from PDF documents (e.g.: bill of materials, scope take-offs and quantities) and deliver in an Excel-compatible format.
We automate repetitive tasks such as sorting engineering documents by systems, commodities, and area.
  • We recently split a vendor iso book with over 500 drawings into individual files, named with the document number from the drawing. We automated the process and completed the task in less than five hours. Our automation can save you days of repetitive work.
  • Weld mapping is an important but time-consuming job. Our software simplifies this work by automatically extracting the field welds and overlaying them on the isometrics. With our automation, we are able to reduce weld mapping time by up to 80%.
  • Whether estimating a job or building a work package, a bill of materials (BOM) is essential. We were given an opportunity to extract the BOM from over a 1000 iso’s; both assembly and field. To complete this task, we used our automated processes to extract the BOM and provided an excel copy. Because the extraction was automated, it was completed in hours with an extremely high degree of accuracy. No matter your discipline, our processes can take days out of your planning or estimating cycles.
